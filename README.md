## Description

Calcusino is a betting game where you, the player, put money behind your guesses and answer number related problems. The numbers flip, fuzz, and dissappear quickly, so think carefully about your bets!

---

## INSTRUCTIONS
Start by seleecting your preferred difficulty.
Choose the amount you wish to wager. Higher wagers equal to a harder game!
In the first act, you simply have to guess a number.
Un the second act, you have to add numbers and choose the correct answer.
You lose when you hit 0 chips.
You can use chips to buy powerups between acts.
---

## Iteration Notes

Post-Playtest: Three changes made based on playtesting
- We made it so the game ends when the player hits 0 chips instead of going into the negatives.
- We added the ability to "buy" power ups with chips.
- We added different levels of difficulty.

Post-Showcase: Two planned improvements
- We are planning to have better visuals and leaning into the corny, Vegas casino vibe.
- We are also planning to add more levels with harder and more fun gameplay, and maybe a narrative component as well.
